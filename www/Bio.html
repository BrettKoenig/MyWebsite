Biography

A LITTLE MORE ABOUT ME

I am a man who is pursuing Jesus, and my future wife, Tricia; who also happens to have a passion for technology and sports. I am committed to not confusing these priorities.

You're probably interested in the technology part of that first statement, so I will continue with that; however, if you would like to hear more about the rest, I'd love to talk.

Technology has always fascinated me. Growing up it was as simple as setting up the Sega and Nintendo 64 by myself so I could sneak in a little more time without my parents knowing. As I grew older, with the internet boom and the release of the smarthpone, the ever increasing bounds on technology and those people who were working to push these bounds grew more and more. Then, once I figured out I could learn how to create websites and applications myself, I was in awe, and have never looked back.

Serving, entertaining, and helping or seeing people's dreams come true have always been passions of mine and through web development I have been able to do all three. I believe this is also where my love for entrepreneurship comes from. The world of entrepreneurship is a fast-paced space (which always keeps things interesting) and everyone is trying to pursue their own dreams.

In short, I love trying to envision the big picture, and then follow through and carry out the small details. If you have a project, no matter how big or small, or just an idea, I would love to hear from you!
